Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

AI PM Summit is operated by CoFi Labs Inc. ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit aipmsummit.com or register for our events.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name and email address when you register for the summit or sign up for updates
  • Your job title and company if provided during registration
  • Any messages you send us via our contact form

We may also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and referring URLs. This is collected through standard web analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you confirmation and event access details for the AI PM Summit
  • Send you updates about the event, speakers, and schedule changes
  • Send you occasional emails about future CoFi events and content (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Respond to your questions or requests
  • Improve our website and event experience

3. Email Communications

By registering for the summit, you agree to receive event-related emails from us. Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate the summit (such as email platforms and registration tools), but only to the extent necessary to provide those services.

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Event Recordings

Sessions at the AI PM Summit are recorded. Speaker sessions may be used for marketing and educational content, as agreed to by speakers. Attendee video or audio is not recorded without consent.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and understand how visitors use our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with event access and follow-up communications, or until you request deletion.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
